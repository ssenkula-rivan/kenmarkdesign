About Kenmark Design

It's all by the Grace of God

Kenmark Design has been serving the Kampala community with exceptional printing and publishing services since 2016. Our commitment to quality, efficiency, and customer satisfaction has made us a trusted partner for individuals, businesses, churches, and educational institutions.

With our state-of-the-art 700 digital color press and experienced team of designers and print professionals, we deliver outstanding results on every project. From small business cards to large format banners, from simple documents to complete book publishing services, we handle it all with the same dedication to excellence.
